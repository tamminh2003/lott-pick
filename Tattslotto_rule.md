# Tattslotto rule

The rules are you pick 6 numbers from 1 to 45.

On the day of the draw 6 main numbers and 2 supplement numbers are drawn.

## How to win:

- 1st division: 6 main numbers

- 2nd division: 5 main numbers and 1 supplement number

- 3rd division: 5 main numbers

- 4th division: 4 main numbers

- 5th division: 3 main numbers and 1 supplement number

- 6th division: 3 main numbers
